WRITING TASK: Renewable Energy Paragraph

Write a paragraph about renewable energy. Your paragraph MUST follow ALL 8 formatting rules below. Failure to follow any rule will result in a deduction.

RULE 1: The paragraph must be exactly 100 words. Not 99, not 101.

RULE 2: The first word of the paragraph must be "Although".

RULE 3: The paragraph must contain exactly 3 sentences.

RULE 4: Every sentence must contain the word "energy" at least once.

RULE 5: No sentence may exceed 45 words.

RULE 6: The paragraph must mention at least 2 specific types of renewable energy (e.g., solar, wind, hydroelectric, geothermal, biomass, tidal).

RULE 7: The paragraph must include exactly one statistic or numerical fact (e.g., a percentage, a year, a capacity figure). Only one number is permitted in the entire paragraph.

RULE 8: The final word of the paragraph must be "future".
